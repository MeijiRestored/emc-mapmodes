Information

On this website you can visualize different statistics directly onto EarthMC's map, by switching map modes with the buttons on the bottom of the page.

Current modes available:
- Default: The regular map, but better looking.
- EU4 Colors: Nations are colored just like in EU4.
- Population: Colors towns depending on thier amount of residents.
- Claims: Colors towns depending on thier amount of chunks claimed.
- PvP status: Shows which towns have PvP enabled.
- Density: Colors towns depending on thier population density.
- Nation Bonus: Colors nations depending on thier nation bonus.
